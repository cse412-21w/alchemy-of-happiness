Health (Life Expectancy) vs Happiness in Map View:

This map view of Happiness Score and Health (Life expentancy) is hoping to give the audience a quick overview of the dataset. The graph implemented a filter on year so that the reader is able to quickly select the desired year and compare the visual differences between them. The country is shaded in the color with respective to their Happiness Score and the color of dot represents its Health facter. As we can see that darker regions under happiness score corresponds with darker shaded dots under life expectancy factor. Therefore, we decided to look further into the relationship between Happiness score and health facter.

Health (Life Expectancy) vs Happiness in Scatter Plot:

In the next step, we tried using a scatter plot to illustrate the relationship between the two variables. We plotted all five years of data with X-axis being Happiness Score and Y-axis being Life Expectancy. Then we divided the points into four sub-sections by high/low life expectancy & high/low happiness score. As we can see that majority of the data is divided into Quadrant I (+,+) and Quadrant III (-,-), which means life expectancy directly relates to its happiness score. More specificly, high life expectancy countries has high happiness score and, conversely, low life expectancy countries has low happiness score.
